Our Story

Woven with Love in the Heart of Kashi

Welcome to Naveera, where tradition meets elegance. Rooted in the ancient city of Varanasi, our brand is a tribute to the timeless heritage of Banarasi craftsmanship. For centuries, the looms of Kashi have produced the finest silks in the world, and we are here to bring that royalty directly to your doorstep.

Naveera was born out of a passion for authentic handloom. We believe that a saree is not just a piece of clothing; it is a legacy passed down through generations. By working directly with local artisans, we ensure that every thread reflects the true spirit of Banaras.

✨ Authenticity

We guarantee 100% genuine Banarasi Silk and handloom products, ethically sourced from local weavers.

🤝 Ethical Sourcing

By removing middlemen, we empower our artisans with fair wages while offering you competitive prices.

📍 Proudly Varanasi

Our operations are based in the holy city of Kashi, ensuring every product carries the authentic vibe of the Ganga Ghats.

🌟 Quality Check

Every saree undergoes a multi-level quality inspection before it is packed for our Naveera family.
