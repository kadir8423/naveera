Returns & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority at Naveera.

📦 7-Day Easy Returns

We want you to love your Banarasi masterpiece. If you are not completely satisfied, you can initiate a return within 7 days of receiving your order.

  • The item must be in its original, unused condition.
  • All original tags, labels, and packaging must be intact.
  • Sarees with falls or edging (pico) done by the customer cannot be returned.

💰 Fast Refunds

Once we receive your returned item and it passes our quality check, your refund will be processed immediately.

  • Refunds are credited to the original payment method (Bank/UPI/Card).
  • The amount will reflect in your account within 5-7 working days.
  • Shipping charges (if any) are non-refundable.

⚠️ Damaged or Incorrect Items

In the rare case that you receive a damaged or wrong product, we offer a 100% Free Replacement.
